THE METHERAPPY FOUNDATION (T.M.F)

Mental Health Awareness and Education

The meTherappy Foundation is committed to promoting mental health awareness and education by reaching out to schools, communities, and underserved groups. Our goal is to break the stigma surrounding mental health, provide access to helpful resources, and empower individuals—especially the youth—with the tools and knowledge to prioritize their mental well-being.

THE METHERAPPY FOUNDATION (T.M.F)

We Are Building The Future Of Tele-health Together, Be With Us

The meTherappy Foundation (T.M.F) is the outreach arm of our platform, dedicated to promoting mental health education, early intervention, and community support. Through school visits, public awareness campaigns, and grassroots initiatives, we aim to break the stigma around mental health, empower young people with the tools to thrive emotionally, and make mental wellness accessible to everyone, everywhere.

THE METHERAPPY FOUNDATION (T.M.F)

We Engage in Outdoor-Sensitization, Exercises and Social-Networking Activities

The Metherappy Foundation promotes mental well-being through outdoor sensitization workshops to raise awareness, inclusive exercise sessions like yoga to boost physical health, and social networking events to foster community connections, all held in natural settings to enhance accessibility and inclusivity.
