About Us

At meTherappy, we believe that everyone deserves access to quality mental health support no matter where they are or what they're going through. We are an online platform built to connect individuals with a wide range of licensed therapists, counselors, and mental health professionals.Whether you're navigating stress,anxiety,relationships,grief,or just need someone to talk to, meTherappy offers a safe, supportive space to find the help you need.
Our mission is simple:
To make therapy accessible, affordable, and tailored to you.With flexible scheduling,affordable pricing,and therapists across various specialties, meTherappy puts you in control of your mental wellness journey—on your own time, in your own way. Because we believe therapy isn't a luxury. It's the therappy you need.
